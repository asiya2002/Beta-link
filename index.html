about us

We Guarantee A Fun Time In A Comfortable And Secure Environment

BETAlink is a company that came into existance with the objective of improving the mental health as well as general wellbeing of students in the university around the country, ranging from the ages of 16-26. Through research we have discovered that most students who do not do well in school do so as a result of the lack of basic social skills essential for every student, as without these social skills it becomes difficult for the student to participate actively in their academics.

Our major aim is to help students undertand that school is not necessarily scary or boring, by engaging them in social events and activities that will help build their social skills as well as stimulate their brain capacity. We hope to reduce the high level of social anxiety, depression, school drop-outs, loss of self-esteem and suicide in extreme cases among students.
